"Manager Members" help
  • List of all members of research units. Get and change the details of each member.
  • A manager is greeted with three lists:
    • With roles: People who have a known affiliation to a team (whether scientific, technical, science management or administrative).
    • Without roles: People whose affiliation is not known. With time this list should tend to a size of zero elements.
    • To validate: New members with pending pre-registrations validation. Check regularly this list. Only members with validated pre-registrations will appear on lists of people.
  • The list of people with roles can be exported to a spreadsheet file by pressing on the spreadsheet icon on the top of the page.
  • To check change the details of a particular person press the icon.
  • In the details tab for each person (appearing to the right of "Without roles") the view and functionalities are similar to what is described in the "Myself" section.
  • The manager can choose a new password for a user in case the user forgets his/her password.
