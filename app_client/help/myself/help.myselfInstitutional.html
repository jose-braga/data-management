"Institutional" help
  • This section is about your affiliations to academic institutions.
  • Institutional contacts: This information will be publicly available on the units websites.
  • Affiliation to Academic Institutions: End date is optional.
  • Pole: Can only be changed by manager.
  • Roles at institution: The user has 4 roles available:
    • Scientific: for those directly involved in research activities
    • Technical: for those who work at facilities that support research
    • Science management: for those who are involved in jobs related with science communication, funding, information systems,etc.
    • Administrative: for those who are involved in administrative jobs.
  • Details for each role are filled in the subsequent tabs. A user can select several roles.
