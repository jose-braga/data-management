"Personal" help
  • This section revolves around your personal information. Most of this information is available only to managers.
  • Name and nuclear information: Colloquial name is a name commonly/informally used to identify person and is the name that will be shown in the website.
    To help you selecting a nationality, there is a search feature at the begining of the selection. It is possible to select multiple nationalities.
  • Personal contact information: enter your address, personal phone and personal email.
  • Emergency contacts: insert here the people to be contacted in case you have an emergency.
  • Website personal photo: photo selection with crop functionality . The crop area can be enlarged or reduced according to your preferences.
  • Identifications: enter the IDs which are relevant to administrative and management procedores in your institution.
