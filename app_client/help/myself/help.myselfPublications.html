"Publications" help
  • List: List of publications where person is author with LAQV or UCIBIO affiliation.
    • Public column - only publications tagged as visible will by shown on external entities (such as the public LAQV or UCIBIO websites).
    • Highlighted column - Selected publications to appear in your personal "Highlighted Publications" in public LAQV or UCIBIO website.
    • If a publication is associated with you by mistake remove it by pressing .
    • Check publication details by pressing .
    • The list of publications can be exported to a spreadsheet file by pressing on the spreadsheet icon.
  • Add: Add publications already in the database or from your ORCID.
    • Within LAQV/UCIBIO database: start search by typing a few characters, and then select your publications.
      Only shows publications that are not associated with you.
    • Search your ORCID: shows a list of your publications not found on LAQV/UCIBIO database.
      Select publications, add some more information and then press Add ORCID publications.
