"Team members" help
  • List of currrent and past team members. Only users with at least the "Team" permission level will be able to reach this info.
  • Users with "Team" permissions can change position, % of dedication and start/end times for each member.
  • There is a search by name feature on the top of the list.
  • The filtered list of people can be exported to a spreadsheet file by pressing on the spreadsheet icon on top.
  • If a person is missing with this list or if someone does not belong to it report to the unit managers
  • Responsibles: person from LAQV and UCIBIO that is responsible for definining your work tasks.
