"Team Publications" help
  • First card presents a list of publications reported by all members of a team.
    These are publications contained in the database. If a publication is missing, you or a team member, must add it to his/her personal publications (in Myself).

    Note: not all publications by your team members should be part of your lab publications (published while in another institution, etc)
  • After selecting publications from first list, press "Add" to add these publications to the list of lab publications.
  • The second card presents the list of lab publications. Similarly to personal publications, you can select some to be part of highlighted publications.
    Lab publications can be hidden from external services by unticking the "Public" field.
