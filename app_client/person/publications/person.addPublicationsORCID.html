These are publications found on ORCID that are not part of LAQV/UCIBIO database yet.
To add publications to the database, the procedure is:

  1. Select publications from "ORCID results".
    Only publications with citations in BibTex format will be automatically parsed. For the remaining, you must enter details manually.
  2. Check/edit publication details (on "Selected publication details").
    Add some more information and then clik + button to add.
  3. Press "Add ORCID pub.".
